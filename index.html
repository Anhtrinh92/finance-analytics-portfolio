▸  Key Insights ▾

With profit margin −26.8%, the platform is making a loss on every order across all payment methods.

Core costs — delivery fee, payment processing fee, discounts & offers — generate a net loss of −5k INR per order before accounting for refunds and chargebacks.

Discounts and offers account for ~56% of core costs. Orders without discounts are profitable, indicating current discount schemes are not effectively structured.

The New User 15% discount generates the largest individual loss (−23k INR), suggesting that aggressive acquisition discounting sacrifices profitability without a clear retention payoff.

High discount rates attract new users but the current rate structure needs review — a targeted discount cap or tiered offer scheme could materially improve margin without reducing acquisition volume.
